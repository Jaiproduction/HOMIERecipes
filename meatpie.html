Meat Pie

Flaky pastry filled with savory minced meat and vegetables

Introduction

Meat pies are a beloved snack in many cultures, especially popular in West Africa. They feature a flaky, golden crust and a deliciously spiced minced meat filling. Perfect as a snack or light meal.

Ingredients

For the pastry:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 250 g cold butter (cubed)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cold water

For the filling:

  • 300 g minced beef
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2 medium carrots (diced)
  • 2 medium potatoes (diced)
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Add butter and rub until crumbly. Add cold water and knead into dough. Chill for 30 minutes.
  2. For filling: heat oil in a pan, sauté onions, add minced beef, cook until brown. Add carrots, potatoes, thyme, and seasoning. Cook until tender, let cool.
  3. Roll out dough, cut circles, fill with meat mixture, fold, and seal edges with a fork.
  4. Brush tops with egg wash and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25–30 minutes until golden brown.
Tip: Seal edges tightly to avoid filling spilling out while baking.

Serving Suggestion

Serve warm with a chilled soft drink or tea. Perfect for parties, picnics, or as an afternoon snack.
