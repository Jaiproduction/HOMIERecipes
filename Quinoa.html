Quinoa & Veggie Bowl

A colorful and nutritious bowl packed with quinoa, fresh veggies, and protein

Introduction

The quinoa & veggie bowl is a healthy, balanced meal loaded with fiber, protein, and vibrant flavors. It’s perfect for lunch or dinner and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 medium carrot, shredded
  • ½ cup chickpeas (cooked or canned)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: In a pot, add rinsed quinoa and water/broth. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 15 minutes until fluffy.
  2. Steam or lightly sauté broccoli until tender-crisp.
  3. Prepare veggies: slice avocado, halve tomatoes, and shred carrot.
  4. In a bowl, arrange quinoa as the base. Top with broccoli, tomatoes, carrot, chickpeas, and avocado.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
Tip: Add grilled chicken, tofu, or boiled eggs for extra protein.

Serving Suggestion

Enjoy as a nourishing one-bowl meal, or serve as a side alongside grilled meats or fish.
