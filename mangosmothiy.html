Mango Smoothie

Thick, creamy, and naturally sweet tropical refreshment

Introduction

A mango smoothie is a tropical delight that’s creamy, refreshing, and naturally sweet. Perfect for breakfast, snacks, or cooling off on a hot day.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes (peeled and diced)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (or milk/plant milk)
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • Mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add mango pieces, yogurt, honey (if using), and ice cubes into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into chilled glasses.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
Tip: For extra creaminess, add a banana or a splash of coconut milk.

Serving Suggestion

Serve cold in tall glasses with a straw. Great as a breakfast drink or mid-day refresher.
