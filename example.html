Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  • Spread tomato sauce on dough.
  • Add mozzarella and basil.
  • Drizzle with olive oil.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes.

Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients:

  • Beef patty
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Burger bun
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Ketchup, mustard

Steps:

  • Grill or pan-fry beef patty.
  • Place cheese on patty to melt.
  • Assemble bun with veggies and sauces.
  • Add patty and serve hot.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion, garlic, herbs
  • Grated parmesan

Steps:

  • Cook pasta until al dente.
  • Sauté onion and garlic.
  • Add beef and cook through.
  • Mix in tomato sauce and simmer.
  • Serve over pasta with cheese.

Salmon Sushi Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Fresh salmon
  • Nori sheets
  • Rice vinegar, sugar
  • Wasabi, soy sauce

Steps:

  • Cook and season rice.
  • Lay rice on nori sheet.
  • Add salmon and roll tightly.
  • Slice into pieces and serve.

Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Grilled chicken
  • Salsa
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  • Grill and shred chicken.
  • Warm tortillas.
  • Assemble tacos with ingredients.
  • Top with salsa and cheese.
