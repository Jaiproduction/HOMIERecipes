Sandwiches

Club, Tuna, and Egg — quick and tasty favorites for any meal

Introduction

Sandwiches are versatile, quick to prepare, and delicious. From the layered Club Sandwich to the refreshing Tuna and the protein-rich Egg Sandwich, these options make perfect snacks or light meals.

Ingredients

Club Sandwich:

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 2 slices cooked chicken or turkey
  • 2 slices bacon
  • 1 slice cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise

Tuna Sandwich:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 small can tuna (drained)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Lettuce

Egg Sandwich:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 boiled eggs (mashed)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Club Sandwich: Toast bread, spread mayonnaise, layer with chicken, bacon, cheese, lettuce, and tomato. Cut into quarters.
  2. Tuna Sandwich: Mix tuna with mayonnaise. Spread on bread with lettuce and close sandwich.
  3. Egg Sandwich: Mash boiled eggs with mayonnaise, salt, and pepper. Spread mixture on bread and serve.
Tip: For extra flavor, toast the bread lightly and add a slice of avocado or cucumber to your sandwiches.

Serving Suggestion

Serve with a side of fries, potato chips, or salad for a complete meal. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a picnic snack.
