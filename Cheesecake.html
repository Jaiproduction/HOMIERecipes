Cheesecake

Creamy, rich, and indulgent — the ultimate dessert

Introduction

Cheesecake is a timeless dessert with a buttery crust and a rich, creamy filling. Perfect for special occasions or anytime you crave a decadent treat.

Ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits (or graham crackers)
  • 100g unsalted butter, melted
  • 500g cream cheese, softened
  • 200g sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C (320°F). Grease a springform pan.
  2. Crush biscuits into fine crumbs and mix with melted butter. Press into pan base to form crust.
  3. In a bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Stir in sour cream and vanilla until fully combined.
  6. Pour mixture over crust and smooth the top.
  7. Bake 50–60 minutes until set but slightly jiggly in the center.
  8. Cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
Tip: Add fresh fruit or a drizzle of caramel or chocolate sauce for extra flavor.

Serving Suggestion

Slice and serve chilled with berries, whipped cream, or a sweet sauce topping.
