Caesar Salad

A classic salad with crisp romaine, creamy dressing, croutons, and parmesan cheese

Introduction

Caesar Salad is a timeless favorite, combining crunchy romaine lettuce with a tangy dressing, crisp croutons, and a sprinkle of parmesan cheese. It’s simple yet elegant, perfect as a starter or light meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 large romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Optional: Grilled chicken or shrimp for protein

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop romaine lettuce, then pat dry.
  2. In a large bowl, toss lettuce with Caesar dressing and minced garlic until evenly coated.
  3. Add croutons and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  4. Top with grilled chicken or shrimp if desired.
  5. Serve immediately while crisp and fresh.
Tip: For homemade croutons, toast bread cubes in olive oil with garlic and herbs until golden.

Serving Suggestion

Serve chilled as a starter, or pair with grilled meats for a complete meal.
