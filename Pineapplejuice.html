Pineapple Juice

Fresh, tropical, and naturally sweet homemade juice

Introduction

Pineapple juice is a refreshing tropical drink that’s naturally sweet and full of vitamin C. It’s easy to make at home and perfect for cooling down on hot days.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe pineapple (peeled and chopped)
  • 2 cups cold water (or coconut water)
  • 1–2 tbsp sugar or honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (for serving)
  • Mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add chopped pineapple and water into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the juice using a fine sieve or cheesecloth (optional, for smoother texture).
  4. Stir in sugar or honey if needed.
  5. Serve chilled over ice cubes.
Tip: Mix with orange juice or ginger for extra flavor.

Serving Suggestion

Serve in tall glasses with ice and a mint garnish. Perfect for summer days or as a party refreshment.
