Avocado Salad

A fresh, creamy, and colorful salad packed with healthy fats and crunchy veggies

Introduction

This avocado salad is light, refreshing, and bursting with flavor. Creamy avocado pairs beautifully with crisp vegetables, making it a healthy side dish or a quick lunch option.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 medium cucumber, chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon or lime juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh coriander or parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut avocados, cucumber, and tomato into bite-sized pieces. Place in a salad bowl.
  2. Add sliced red onion and chopped herbs.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon/lime juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Toss gently to combine without mashing the avocado.
  5. Serve immediately for the best freshness.
Tip: Add feta cheese or grilled chicken to make it more filling.

Serving Suggestion

Perfect as a side dish for grilled meats, or enjoy it on its own with crusty bread.
