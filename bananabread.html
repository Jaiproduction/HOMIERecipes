Banana Bread

Moist, sweet, and packed with ripe banana flavor

Introduction

Banana bread is a classic comfort bake — soft, moist, and naturally sweetened with ripe bananas. Perfect for breakfast, snacks, or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, mash bananas and mix with melted butter.
  3. Stir in sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla.
  4. Add baking soda, salt, and flour. Mix until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for 50–60 minutes.
  6. Cool before slicing and serving.
Tip: Add chocolate chips or chopped walnuts for extra flavor and texture.

Serving Suggestion

Enjoy warm with butter, or serve with tea or coffee for a cozy treat.
