Pancakes & Syrup

Fluffy golden pancakes drizzled with sweet syrup

Introduction

Soft, fluffy pancakes topped with syrup are a breakfast classic. Easy to make and always satisfying, they’re perfect for mornings with family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Syrup (maple or honey) for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir until just mixed (batter should be slightly lumpy).
  4. Heat a nonstick pan and lightly grease with butter or oil.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  6. Stack pancakes and drizzle generously with syrup.
Tip: Add blueberries, bananas, or chocolate chips to the batter for variety.

Serving Suggestion

Serve warm with syrup, fresh fruit, or a dollop of whipped cream.
