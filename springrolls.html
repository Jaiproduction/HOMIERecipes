Spring Rolls

Crunchy rolls filled with vegetables and meat, fried to golden perfection

Introduction

Spring rolls are a popular appetizer made with thin pastry wrappers filled with vegetables and sometimes meat. Fried until crispy, they are enjoyed with sweet chili or soy dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 20 spring roll wrappers
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or shrimp (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan, sauté garlic, then add cabbage, carrots, bean sprouts, and soy sauce. Stir-fry for 3–4 minutes, let cool.
  2. Place 2 tbsp of filling on a wrapper, fold sides, and roll tightly. Seal edges with a little water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry rolls in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
Tip: To make them healthier, bake at 200°C (400°F) for 20 minutes instead of frying.

Serving Suggestion

Serve with sweet chili sauce, soy sauce, or peanut dipping sauce. Perfect as an appetizer or party snack.
