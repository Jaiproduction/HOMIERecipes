Zobo Drink (Hibiscus Tea)

A refreshing, tangy, and spiced West African hibiscus drink

Introduction

Zobo is a popular West African beverage made from dried hibiscus petals, flavored with ginger, cloves, and pineapple. It’s refreshing, healthy, and perfect for hot days.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried hibiscus petals (zobo leaves)
  • 1 medium pineapple (peeled and cut in chunks)
  • 1 thumb-sized fresh ginger (peeled)
  • 2–3 cloves
  • 8 cups water
  • Sugar or honey (to taste)
  • Slices of lemon or orange (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the dried hibiscus petals to remove dust.
  2. In a large pot, add hibiscus, ginger, cloves, pineapple chunks, and water.
  3. Boil for 20–30 minutes until flavors are well extracted.
  4. Remove from heat, allow to cool slightly, then strain into a bowl or jug.
  5. Add sugar or honey to taste and stir until dissolved.
  6. Refrigerate and serve chilled with ice cubes.
Tip: Add a splash of orange juice or lime for extra tanginess.

Serving Suggestion

Serve cold in tall glasses with lemon or orange slices. Great for parties and family gatherings.
