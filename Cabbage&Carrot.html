Cabbage & Carrot Slaw

A crunchy, colorful slaw with a creamy dressing — simple and refreshing

Introduction

This cabbage & carrot slaw is crisp, refreshing, and full of flavor. It’s quick to prepare and makes the perfect side dish for barbecues, fried foods, or light meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups green cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 cup purple cabbage, finely shredded (optional)
  • 1 large carrot, grated
  • ½ small red onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar or honey
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine shredded cabbage, carrot, and onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk mayonnaise, vinegar/lemon juice, sugar/honey, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Pour dressing over the vegetables and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes before serving for best flavor.
Tip: For a lighter version, replace mayonnaise with Greek yogurt.

Serving Suggestion

Pairs perfectly with grilled meats, burgers, fried chicken, or as a topping for sandwiches and wraps.
