Garlic Bread

Buttery, garlicky bread that’s crispy on the outside and soft inside

Introduction

Garlic bread is a classic side dish that pairs perfectly with pasta, soups, or grilled dishes. It’s quick to make and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette or Italian bread loaf
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Slice the bread loaf in half lengthwise.
  3. In a bowl, mix butter, garlic, parsley, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Spread the mixture evenly over the bread halves.
  5. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top if using.
  6. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden and crispy.
  7. Slice into pieces and serve warm.
Tip: For extra crispiness, broil the bread for 1–2 minutes after baking.

Serving Suggestion

Serve with pasta dishes like spaghetti bolognese, soups, or enjoy as a snack with marinara dipping sauce.
