Milkshakes

Classic, creamy shakes in vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry flavors

Introduction

Milkshakes are a timeless treat made with ice cream, milk, and flavorings. Whether you love vanilla, chocolate, or strawberry, these shakes are creamy, refreshing, and perfect for any time of the day.

Ingredients

Base (for 2 servings each):

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • Whipped cream (for topping, optional)

Flavor options:

  • Vanilla: 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Chocolate: 1/4 cup chocolate syrup
  • Strawberry: 1/2 cup fresh or frozen strawberries

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine ice cream, milk, and your chosen flavor ingredient.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and top with whipped cream if desired.
  4. Serve immediately with a straw.
Tip: Add crushed cookies, nuts, or chocolate chips for extra fun textures.

Serving Suggestion

Serve cold in tall glasses with straws. Great as a dessert, snack, or party drink.
