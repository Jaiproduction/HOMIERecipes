Shawarma

Juicy spiced meat wrapped in flatbread with veggies and sauces

Introduction

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern street food favorite made with marinated meat, grilled to perfection, and wrapped in flatbread with vegetables and creamy sauces. It's flavorful, filling, and customizable to your taste.

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken or beef (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 4 pita breads or flatbreads
  • Lettuce, tomato, cucumber (sliced)
  • Garlic sauce, tahini, or mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Marinate meat with yogurt, olive oil, garlic, and spices for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  2. Grill or pan-fry the marinated meat until cooked and slightly charred.
  3. Warm pita or flatbread. Spread with garlic sauce or tahini.
  4. Add meat, lettuce, tomato, and cucumber. Roll tightly into a wrap.
  5. Serve warm, wrapped in foil or parchment paper.
Tip: Add pickles or hot sauce for extra flavor and spice.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with fries, coleslaw, or a side salad. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or street-food cravings.
