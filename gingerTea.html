Ginger Tea

Warm, soothing, and spicy tea with health-boosting benefits

Introduction

Ginger tea is a comforting drink made from fresh ginger root. Known for its warming flavor and medicinal properties, it’s perfect for digestion, colds, and relaxation.

Ingredients

  • 1 thumb-sized piece fresh ginger (peeled and sliced)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1–2 tsp honey (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a small pot.
  2. Add sliced ginger and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Strain into cups.
  4. Add honey and lemon juice if desired.
  5. Stir and serve hot.
Tip: Add a cinnamon stick or cloves for extra spice and warmth.

Serving Suggestion

Best enjoyed hot on chilly mornings or evenings, or whenever you need a soothing boost.
