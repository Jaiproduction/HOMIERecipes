Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty Italian pasta classic with rich, meaty sauce

Introduction

Spaghetti Bolognese is a beloved Italian dish featuring pasta served with a slow-simmered tomato and beef sauce. Comforting, flavorful, and perfect for weeknight dinners or special family meals.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté onions, garlic, carrots, and celery until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Reduce heat and let sauce simmer for 20–25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve sauce over spaghetti, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
Tip: For extra depth of flavor, add a splash of red wine while simmering the sauce.

Serving Suggestion

Pair with garlic bread and a fresh green salad for a complete meal.
