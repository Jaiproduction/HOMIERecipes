Roasted Potatoes

Crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside — the perfect potato side

Introduction

Roasted potatoes are a simple yet delicious side dish. With a crispy golden crust and fluffy inside, they pair well with almost any main meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary or thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Boil potato chunks in salted water for 5 minutes, then drain.
  3. Toss potatoes with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  5. Roast for 35–40 minutes, turning halfway through, until golden and crispy.
Tip: For extra crispiness, shake the potatoes after boiling to roughen the edges before roasting.

Serving Suggestion

Perfect as a side dish for roasted chicken, steak, or grilled vegetables.
