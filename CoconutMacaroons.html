Coconut Macaroons

Chewy, sweet, and coconut-packed little delights

Introduction

Coconut macaroons are golden, chewy cookies made with shredded coconut. They’re quick to prepare, naturally gluten-free, and perfect for coconut lovers.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups shredded coconut
  • 2/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large egg whites
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine coconut, condensed milk, and vanilla.
  3. In another bowl, beat egg whites with salt until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold egg whites into the coconut mixture.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of batter onto baking sheet.
  6. Bake 15–20 minutes, until golden brown at the edges.
Tip: Drizzle or dip macaroons in melted chocolate for extra indulgence.

Serving Suggestion

Enjoy as a snack with tea, coffee, or serve as a sweet treat after meals.
