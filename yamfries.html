Yam Fries

Golden, crispy fried yam — a classic West African street food snack

Introduction

Yam fries are a beloved snack and side dish in many parts of Africa. They’re crispy on the outside, soft inside, and often enjoyed with fried eggs, pepper sauce, or ketchup.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium tuber of yam
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: ground chili powder or seasoning cubes

Instructions

  1. Peel the yam and cut into long fry-shaped strips.
  2. Rinse in water and pat dry with a clean towel.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  4. Fry yam strips in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Remove and drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with salt immediately.
Tip: For extra flavor, season the yam strips with a little paprika or garlic powder before frying.

Serving Suggestion

Best enjoyed hot with spicy pepper sauce, fried eggs, or simply ketchup.
