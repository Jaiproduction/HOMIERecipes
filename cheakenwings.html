Chicken Wings

Juicy, crispy wings coated in flavorful spices or sauces

Introduction

Chicken wings are a crowd favorite — crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside, and packed with flavor. Whether baked, fried, or grilled, they’re perfect for parties, game nights, or a fun family meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken wings
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder (optional for heat)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce or hot sauce (for coating)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Pat chicken wings dry with a paper towel. Place in a bowl and toss with oil, garlic powder, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Arrange wings in a single layer on the tray. Bake for 40–45 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.
  4. Remove from oven, toss wings in BBQ or hot sauce, and serve immediately.
Tip: For extra-crispy wings, bake on a wire rack so heat circulates evenly.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with dipping sauces like ranch, blue cheese, or extra BBQ. Pairs well with fries, celery sticks, or a cold drink.
