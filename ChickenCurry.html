Chicken Curry

A fragrant, comforting dish with tender chicken simmered in spiced curry sauce

Introduction

Chicken Curry is a delicious dish enjoyed worldwide, made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich, spiced curry sauce. It’s flavorful, warming, and perfect with rice or bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped (or 1 cup canned tomatoes)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup coconut milk or plain yogurt
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder. Toast the spices lightly.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften into a thick sauce.
  5. Add chicken pieces, season with salt, and stir to coat with spices and sauce.
  6. Pour in coconut milk (or yogurt). Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 25–30 minutes, until chicken is tender and sauce is thickened.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
Tip: For extra flavor, marinate the chicken in curry powder and yogurt for at least 30 minutes before cooking.

Serving Suggestion

Best served with steamed basmati rice, naan bread, or chapati. Add a side of cucumber raita for cooling contrast.
